Nirmala Sitharaman

Nirmala Sitharaman is an Indian economist and politician serving as the Minister of Finance and Corporate Affairs of India since 2019. She is the second woman to hold the office after Indira Gandhi, and she plays a crucial role in India's financial and economic policies.
